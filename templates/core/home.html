About

LPU Connect is a private social networking platform built exclusively for university students to connect, communicate, and collaborate within their campus community. The platform allows students to register using their university email, create personalized profiles, discover classmates, send connection requests, and engage in direct conversations. By ensuring that only verified university members can join, LPU Connect provides a secure and focused environment for meaningful academic and social interaction. Whether you want to expand your network, reconnect with batchmates, explore shared interests, or start a conversation, LPU Connect makes it simple and accessible. This platform is designed to strengthen campus relationships, encourage collaboration, and create a digital space where students truly belong.
